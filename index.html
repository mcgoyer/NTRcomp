About us

Providing small businesses with the knowledge to make sound IT decisions. Not sure how or what to do with your computer systems? We will help guide you through upgrades, purchases, configurations, and standard user support. It's our goal to work towards minimal downtime. All computers will fail at some point its just good practice to have a plan in place to deal with the potential problems before they break.

We deal with a wide varitey of computer issues such as virus removals, VPNs, wired/wireless infostructures, routers, servers (physical/virtual), desktops. We can help you plan your backup strategy, setup redundancies, etc

NTR is located in sunny Vero Beach, FL and was founded by Rob McGoye in 2002. prior to NTR Rob did database programing for a hosting company, setup wireless, DSL, T1, ISDN, Cable Internet connections, 2nd level telephone support for remote control program.
